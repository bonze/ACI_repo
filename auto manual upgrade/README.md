Auto Manual Upgrade
===================

This is a simple script to perform a manual upgrade on multiple 
switches in your ACI (Application Centric Infrastructure) fabric.

<br>
##### Requires: #####
Paramiko >= 1.13+ ( >=1.7.5+ if Python2)  
     
<br>  
##### Supports: #####
Cisco ACI - Up to 12 switches at a time 
<br>
